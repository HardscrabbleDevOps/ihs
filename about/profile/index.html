Integrated Hygiene Systems

Company Profile

Background

Integrated Hygiene Systems (IHS) was established in the year 2009. The company boasts of a technically sound, dedicated, hardworking and determined team, with great passion to improve the general well being of communities and the environment at large by provision of better hygiene services and products. The company has offices located in Harare, Bulawayo and Kwekwe.

General Business Activities

Integrated Hygiene Systems (IHS) is a wholly Zimbabwean owned Private Company which manufactures and distributes a wide range of cleaning, sanitizing, disinfection products and hygiene support services to a wide spectrum of industries in Zimbabwe. IHS is also a Standards Association of Zimbabwe (SAZ), Quality Management System ISO 9001:2015 registered supplier.

IHS Affiliation

Kemklean Hygiene Solutions

IHS has the exclusive distributorship of Kemklean hygiene products in Zimbabwe. Kemklean Hygiene Solutions is a South African based manufacturing company ISO 9001:2008 registered supplier whose products carry the SABS accreditation mark. This therefore assures our customers of consistent high quality at all times

Mandlachem

IHS also has the exclusive distributorship of Mandlachem hygiene products, a reputable supplier of industrial bulk detergents in South Africa. Mandlachem has been manufacturing and supplying conveyor lubricants and CIP detergents to mainly the South African Brewer Plants as well as dairies in South Africa for over 22 years.
