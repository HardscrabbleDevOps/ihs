Brighton Chikosha is a Marketing and Sales Executive for Integrated Hygiene Services. He manages and services segments in Food and Beverages, Healthcare, Hatcheries, Transport, Farm Bio security , animal husbandry hygiene, mining, Dairy and general industrial hygiene . He works closely with I.H.S specialists to deliver comprehensive solutions for everyday hygiene and safety issues. He has extensive experience in various positions in the chemical manufacturing industry. Brighton had a short term with MultiKlean a hygiene chemical company after which he became a Territory Manager at Cernol Chemicals (2013) focusing on Leather Tanning, FMCG ,Laundry and Dry Cleaning , Food and Beverages and Dairy industries. Brighton graduated from Midlands State University, with a B .Com in Marketing Management Honours degree and a Group Diploma with London Chamber of Commerce International (LCCI) in Selling and Sales Management. He is currently studying for a Diploma in Science Technology majoring in Applied Chemistry with Harare Polytechnic College.
