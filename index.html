The IHS story so far...

Integrated Hygiene Systems (IHS) was established in the year 2009. The company boasts of a technically sound, dedicated, hardworking and determined team, with great passion to improve the general well being of communities and the environment at large by provision of better hygiene services and products. The company has offices located in Harare, Bulawayo and Kwekwe.

IHS is actively involved in supplying the following industries:

Food Processing

Beef Industry

Bottling/Brewing

Mines & Workshops

Dairy

Commercial Laundries

Healthcare

Fishery

Poultry

Supermarkets & Restaurants

Crocodile Breeding

Schools
